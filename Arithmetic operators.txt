If a=10, b=20
"+" - Add two objects together  a+b=30
"-" - Get a negative number or a number minus another number  a-b=-10 or b-a=10
"*" - Multiply two numbers or return a string that has been repeated several times  a*b=200
"/" - One number divided by another a/b=0.5 or b/a=2
"%" - Returns the remainder of the division b%a=10
"//" - Returns the integer portion of the quotient (rounded down) 9//2=4 or -9//2=-5
"**" - Returns x to the yth power a**b=100000000000000000000
